About Me

I am currently pursuing my PhD at the Centre for Computational and Data Sciences, IIT Kharagpur, working in the TuRbulent Interfaces And Dispersion (TRIAD) lab under the guidance of Prof. Rajaram Lakkaraju . My research primarily focuses on the intersection of Geophysical Fluid Dynamics (GFD) and Data-driven modeling, with the aim of uncovering the fundamental mechanisms that govern the behavior of planetary atmospheres and oceans. Utilizing reduced order models, I aim to glean insightful observations from extensive datasets, enhancing our understanding of the underlying physical processes that happen across a wide spectrum of scales.
